200g Almond Flour
30g apf or bread flour
200g sugar
100g EVOO
5 eggs.
orange zest
vanilla extract.


eggs + sugar -> whisk the snot out of it. add vanilla and zest.

add the flours.  continue to whisk it up. 

oil a 9" round, oil it up and parchment paper the bottom. 


cook at 320F for 40-50m until a knife runs clean.

frost or not, that's up to you, idaf.
